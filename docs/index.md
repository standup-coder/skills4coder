## About Tech Skills Tree

Tech Skills Tree provides structured learning paths for technology professionals across different roles and specializations. Each skill tree is organized by experience level:

- **Beginner**: For those starting in the field
- **Intermediate**: For professionals building on foundational skills
- **Advanced**: For experienced practitioners tackling complex challenges
- **Expert**: For senior professionals in leadership and strategic roles

The skill trees are designed to be comprehensive yet customizable, allowing professionals to identify relevant skills based on their role, specialization, and career goals.
